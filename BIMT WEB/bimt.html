Greeting and warm welcome in Budaun Institute Of Management & Technology (BIMT). Stellar accomplishments expand the frontiers of knowledge and scholarship every day. In era of globalization of education the obvious focus is on the quality of education. There is no single yardstick of quality. A good educational institution strives and enhancement of quality in every field of its activity.Mission of BIMT, is to dedicate this year to correct understanding of what education really means and let us all benefit from this understanding.
